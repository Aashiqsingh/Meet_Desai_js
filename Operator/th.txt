There are three operator 
1. unary operator 
    - it will required only one operands
2. binary operator
    - it will required two operands
3. ternary operator
    - it will required three operands



Binary operator :
1. Airthmatic operator : + , - , * , / , % , **  
2. assignment operator : += , -= , *= , /= , %= , **= , =
3. relational operator : == , != , <= , >= , === , !==
4. logical operator  : && , || , !
5. bitwise operator : & , | , ^
